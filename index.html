🎥 AI Video Engine

Free · Auto captions, smart cuts, color grade · 10x faster

What used to take 3 hours now takes 3 minutes. AI handles captions, cuts dead space, color-grades, and adds effects. You focus on ideas.

🔗 Get the tools

✂️ Download CapCut Free → 📝 Descript (Free Tier)

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1

Download CapCut Desktop

CapCut is free. Includes auto-captions (95%+ accurate), background removal, AI color grading, and one-click platform templates.

2

Import & auto-caption

Drop footage in. Click "Text" → "Auto captions." Captions boost watch time 40%+.

3

Smart cuts & pacing

Use "Remove silence" to cut dead air. For Shorts, keep clips under 3 seconds. CapCut templates handle transitions and motion graphics.

4

For talking-head: try Descript

Descript edits video like a document — delete words from the transcript, they vanish from the video. Free tier: 1 hour transcription/month.

💡 Pro Tip

Sweet spot for Shorts: 15–30 seconds. Hook in first 1.5 seconds. Pattern interrupts every 2–3 seconds. CapCut templates handle this automatically.
