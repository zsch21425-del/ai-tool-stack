📋 Step-by-Step Setup

1

Download Wispr

Go to wispr.ai and download the desktop app. Available for Mac (Apple Silicon + Intel) and Windows. Install takes 30 seconds. Create a free account — no credit card required for the free tier.

2

Set Your Activation Key

Open Wispr preferences. Set a shortcut you'll remember — I use Ctrl+Shift+Space (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Space (Mac). This key combo opens the dictation mic anywhere on your system.

3

Start Dictating

Click into any text field — Telegram message box, email compose, Google Doc, anywhere. Hit your activation key. Start talking. Wispr transcribes in real time and inserts the text where your cursor is. Punctuation works naturally — just say "comma," "period," "new line."

4

Use It With Your AI Assistant

Open Telegram → click the message box → hit your Wispr shortcut → dictate your message to Hermes. Hands-free AI commands. This is how I send long prompts without typing a single word. Combined with Telegram voice notes for Hermes' responses, you can have full AI conversations without touching your keyboard.

💡 Pro Tip:

Speak in complete sentences with natural pauses. Wispr is context-aware — it gets more accurate the more you use it. For coding or technical terms, just say them clearly and Wispr adapts. The free tier gives 1,000 dictations/month (about 33/day). If you're dictating all day, Pro is $12/month for unlimited. Pair this with the $3 AI Assistant stack and you can run your entire AI workflow by voice.
