🤖 The $3 AI Assistant

$3/month total · 10-minute setup · Runs from your phone

This is the exact stack I use every day. It handles email, research, content creation, and scheduling — all from Telegram. You talk to it like a person. It costs less than a coffee.

🔗 Get the tools

🚀 Install Hermes Agent → 🧠 Get DeepSeek API → 📱 Telegram (Free)

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1

Install Hermes Agent

Open your terminal and run the one-line installer from hermes-agent.nousresearch.com. Takes ~90 seconds. Works on Mac, Linux, and Windows WSL.

2

Get a DeepSeek API key

Sign up at platform.deepseek.com — free credits included. Create an API key. The V4 Pro model is $0.28 per million tokens. Most people spend $2–5/month total.

3

Create a Telegram bot

Message @BotFather on Telegram. Type /newbot, pick a name, and save the token it gives you. Takes 2 minutes.

4

Wire it up & start chatting

Run hermes setup, paste your tokens when prompted, then hermes gateway start. Open Telegram, find your bot, and say "Hey." Your AI assistant is live. Try: "Search for the latest AI news and summarize it."

💡 Pro Tip

Use DeepSeek V4 Pro (not the free Chat model). It handles multi-step tasks reliably. My entire month costs ~$3. If you're on a tight budget, DeepSeek Chat is free and still solid for simple tasks.

🎬 AI Clone Studio

From $5 · Record once, generate forever · 1080p output

Record a 2–3 minute video of yourself. HeyGen creates a digital clone. Type any script — your clone delivers it on video. No camera, lighting, or reshoots. I use this for YouTube Shorts — 10 videos in an afternoon.

🔗 Get the tools

🎭 Try HeyGen Free → 🎙️ ElevenLabs Voice Cloning

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1

Create your HeyGen account

Sign up at HeyGen.com. Start with the Creator plan ($29/month) or use the free trial to test it.

2

Record your avatar video

Film a 2–3 minute video: good lighting, neutral background, look at camera, speak naturally. Upload under "Instant Avatar." Processing takes ~2 hours.

3

Generate videos from text

Type any script into HeyGen. Your clone delivers it. Export as 1080p MP4. Each generation takes ~60 seconds. Create a week of content in one sitting.

4

Optional: Clone your voice

Use ElevenLabs voice cloning ($5/month). Record 3 minutes of audio, it creates an AI voice indistinguishable from yours. Perfect for voiceovers without recording.

💡 Pro Tip

Record in 4K with front lighting. Busy backgrounds and rapid head movements reduce quality. A 3-minute recording with varied expressions produces the most realistic clone.

🎥 AI Video Engine

Free · Auto captions, smart cuts, color grade · 10x faster

What used to take 3 hours now takes 3 minutes. AI handles captions, cuts dead space, color-grades, and adds effects. You focus on ideas.

🔗 Get the tools

✂️ Download CapCut Free → 📝 Descript (Free Tier)

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1

Download CapCut Desktop

CapCut is free. The desktop version includes auto-captions (95%+ accurate), background removal, AI color grading, and one-click platform templates.

2

Import & auto-caption

Drop your footage in. Click "Text" → "Auto captions." CapCut transcribes and syncs captions automatically. Captions boost watch time 40%+.

3

Smart cuts & pacing

Use "Remove silence" to cut dead air. For Shorts, keep clips under 3 seconds each. CapCut's templates handle transitions, zooms, and motion graphics.

4

For talking-head: try Descript

Descript edits video like a document — delete words from the transcript and they vanish from the video. Free tier: 1 hour transcription/month.

💡 Pro Tip

Sweet spot for Shorts: 15–30 seconds. Hook in first 1.5 seconds. Pattern interrupts every 2–3 seconds. CapCut templates handle this automatically.
